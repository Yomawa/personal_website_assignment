Zdravo, I am Maja

I'm a native Serbian, but have been living and working in San Francisco for the last six years. I received a Bachelor's Degree in computer science and business from Belgrade Business School, but moved to San Francisco to fulfill a lifelong dream of becoming a visual designer. After mastering English, I began studying graphic and web design at the City College of San Francisco.

Working as professional graphic designer for past couple of years inspired me to start learning to code. I wanted to not just design mockups for websites but also to be able to develop them myself.

I am excited to be a gSchool student and learn about web technologies like javascript and ruby on rails. My hope is to achieve my goal of become a successful software and web developer and become a contributing factor to this field by creating useful programs and applications that can help other people on their paths through life. I've always been fascinated by the web's ability to bring people together, after gSchool I would love to work in the travel industry for companies like Orbitz or TripAdvisor.

When I'm not glued to my computer discovering new ways to use Adobe Suite, you can find me
rock climbing, enjoying the happy baby pose in yoga class, or traveling the world and getting inspiration for new designs!
